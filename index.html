ちいかわ

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Chiikawa (ちいかわ), also known as Chiisakute Kawaii Yatsu (なんか小さくてかわいいやつ, "Something Small and Cute"), is a Japanese manga series by Nagano. It has been serialized online via Twitter since January 2020 and has been collected in five tankōbon volumes by Kodansha. An anime television series adaptation by Doga Kobo premiered in April 2022.





Main Characters

Chiikawa(ちいかわ) / CV. Haruka Aoki

            The main character of Chiikawa, who is a white hamster.
            Very shy and timid in terms of personality, but also incredibly kind hearted.

Hachiware(ハチワレ) / CV. Masato Tanaka

            One of Chiikawa's bestfriend, who is a tuxedo cat with blue patterns.
            Has a friendly and optimistic personality, and loves caring for others.

Usagi(うさぎ) / CV. Ari Ozawa

            Chiikawa's another bestfriend, who is a yellow bunny.
            Always full of energy and hypered, but cheers up friends with happiness.

Momonga(モモンガ) / CV. Yuka Iguchi

            A flying squirrel with blue ears and tail, who seeks attention and love.
            The cheeky attitude gets irritating sometimes, but you can never hate it.

Rakko(ラッコ) CV. Yuma Uchida

            A Sea otter that is able to fiercly fight, unlike how adorable it looks like.
            It's personality is very bold and calm, as it often helps out Chiikawa and friends.

Kuri-Manju(くりまんじゅう) CV. Takayuki Asai

            Creature that is motivated from chestnuts and otters.
            Likes to drink alchohol and always has a tough manner.

Shisa(シーサー) / CV. Shimabukuro Miyuri

            Motived from a guardian animal in Okinawa, Japan, who works in a ramen shop.
            It has a strong tenacity and a sincere personality.

Kani(カニ) [1]

            An unknown creature that seems to look like a crab, who gets along with Momonga.
            Always sweet and gets happy easily.
